Our Story

A Taste of Nepal
in Scarborough

Falcha was born from a love of Newari food — the ancient, rich culinary tradition of the Newar people of the Kathmandu Valley. We bring those bold, fermented, and spiced flavors to Scarborough with fresh ingredients sourced locally.

Whether you're discovering Newari food for the first time or craving a taste of home, Falcha welcomes you with warmth, authenticity, and community spirit.
